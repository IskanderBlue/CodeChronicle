# 5.1 — Mobile Transition Compare

## What

Adapt transition compare cards for small screens.

## Why

Side-by-side desktop compare panes do not fit on mobile.

## Changes

- Replace desktop side-by-side compare with a mobile tab switcher.
- Tabs should allow switching between old/new versions.
- Preserve the transition banner above the version content.
- If a diff-highlight mode is explored, keep it behind an explicit scope check;
  do not make it required for completion.

## Completion Conditions

- Transition compare cards are usable on narrow screens.
- Old/new switching works without stacking full versions vertically.
- Banner content remains visible and readable.

## How to Test

### Browser/manual test: `mobile_transition_compare_uses_tabs_instead_of_stacked_versions`

Viewport:

- iPhone SE width or similar narrow viewport

Expected:

- Old/New tabs are present.
- Only one version pane is visible at a time.
- Transition banner remains visible.

## Depends On

- 3.3 transition compare cards.

## Notes

- Diff mode is optional and not part of completion criteria.
