# Pricing

This product is currently in **early access**.

The goal right now is to validate real-world engineering use cases and ensure accuracy, coverage, and speed before a full commercial launch.

---

## Anonymous Access
**Free**
- Up to **3 searches per day**
- No account required
- Intended for quick lookups and evaluation

---

## Early Access (Signed Up)
**Free (for now)**
- **Unlimited searches**
- Ontario Building Code editions available from **2003-09-01 to present**
- Other codes from [Canada_building_code_mcp](https://github.com/DavidCho1999/Canada_building_code_mcp)
- Results include exact sections and edition context

**Notes**
- This is not yet a substitute for owning the official code publications
- Use for research and discovery, not final authority
- Coverage is incomplete & expanding
