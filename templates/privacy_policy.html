Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This website ("the Service"), operated by CodeChronicle Corp. ("we", "us"), provides search and reference access to building codes and related regulatory materials.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (if you create an account)

Usage Data

  • Search queries
  • Pages and code sections viewed
  • Timestamps and frequency of use
  • Basic technical information (IP address, browser type)

This data is used to improve coverage, accuracy, and performance of the Service. Search queries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to interpret them into structured search parameters; under our agreement with Anthropic, they are not used to train AI models.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. Payment details such as card numbers are provided directly to Stripe and are never stored on our servers; we retain only your subscription status and billing metadata. Stripe's handling of your information is described in its own privacy policy.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Service
  • Improve search results and coverage
  • Understand how the Service is used
  • Communicate about access, updates, or support requests

We do not sell personal data.

Data Sharing

We may share data:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the Service, currently including Google Cloud (hosting), Neon (database hosting), Stripe (payment processing), and Anthropic (search query interpretation)
  • If required by law

Aggregated or anonymized usage data may be used for analytics and reporting.

Where Data Is Stored

The Service is hosted on infrastructure located in the United States. Your information may be stored and processed outside Canada and may be subject to the laws of the jurisdictions where it is held.

Cookies

The Service uses only essential cookies — the session and security cookies needed for sign-in and form protection. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Data Retention

We retain account and usage data as long as necessary to operate and improve the Service, unless deletion is requested.

Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data

Limitations

This Service is provided for research and informational purposes only and does not replace official code publications.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@codechronicle.ca.
