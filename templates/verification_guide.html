Reading the verification rail

What the marks on the in-force band mean, and the status it reports

{% rail_legend as legend %}

Every search result carries an in-force band whose body is the verification rail: it times the provision you are reading against the e-Laws consolidations that can independently vouch for it. The bar runs from the version’s in-force start (left) to its end — or current (right); your query date sits on it as a tick. How much of the bar is solid, and what marks sit on it, say how directly the text is confirmed on that date.
